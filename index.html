About me

My name is Marek (Mark), born in 92 in Poland. I'm an avid gamer, video game afficionado. Just kidding, average K/D and all that, but I do love video games.

My coding journey began circa mid 2019 or so, tumbling around codecamp and digital spaces alike. First Udemy course really kickstarted it all, allowing me to feel more or less confident with CSS. After that, another Udemy course, now another, and more after that.

It's something I'd gladly do for a living, hence this portfolio. I hope it helps me land my junior job.
